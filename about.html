Who I Am

Hi, I'm Muhammad Abdullah.

I'm a Gaming and Animation student at the University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Lahore, currently in the early stages of my degree with two semesters completed. I spend most of my time turning ideas into working software — websites, AI-powered tools, and small games.

I like projects that force me to connect different pieces together: a frontend that feels good to use, a database that holds up, and — increasingly — a bit of AI that makes the whole thing smarter. Whether it's an event-planning platform, a fitness website, or a system that screens resumes automatically, I care about building things that actually work end to end, not just look good in a screenshot.

Outside of coursework, I build my own projects to learn faster than a classroom alone allows — picking up new stacks like Flask, MySQL, and AI/NLP libraries along the way.
